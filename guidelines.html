SpekCheck

Guidelines

Capitalization

We follow RateYourMusic's standards for capitalization. The exception is non-standard capitalization with artistic intent.

References

Include a link to a music database to verify the existence of the release you're uploading. Some databases you may reference:

Discogs
RateYourMusic
MusicBrainz
VocaDB
Encyclopedia Metallum
AllMusic

Sources

Mention the website, tracker or method through which you obtained the audio files. We expect you to be honest, as this helps users discern sources with lossy transcodes. It's okay to leave this empty if you don't know the source.

Uploads

All lossless and lossy audio files are allowed. Providing cue/log files for CD rips is appreciated, as it helps verify the quality of your upload. For vinyl rips, include the lineage (equipment used to rip the record). Your audio files will not be stored after being spectographed and verified (okay, FBI?).
